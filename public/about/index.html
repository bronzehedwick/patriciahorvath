About

Patricia Horvath is the author of the short story collection But Now Am Found (Black Lawrence Press) and the memoir All the Difference (Etruscan Press). Her stories and essays have been published widely in literary journals including Shenandoah, Confrontation, New Ohio Review, and The Massachusetts Review. She is the recipient of New York Foundation for the Arts Fellowships in both fiction and literary nonfiction, the Goldenberg Fiction Prize at Bellevue Literary Review, and the Frank Oâ€™Connor Fiction Award.
